QuIT

I noticed an issue when my friends and I played music - we would always be huddled around a laptop looking at chords. Starting at a screen while playing live music is the exact opposite of what a crowd comes to see. For those who don't have the time to memorized a full 2 hour set of songs, how can you get the best of both worlds?


This Apple Watch app helps an artist have the benefit of seeing a chord progression while not having to look at a tablet, laptop, or sheets of music during their performance. Simply turn your Apple Watch to the inside of your wrist and peek when you need to.
